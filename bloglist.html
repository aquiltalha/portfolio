Chandrayaan-3: India lunar rover Pragyaan takes a walk on the Moon

August 2023

India's Moon rover has taken first steps on the lunar surface a day after the country made history by becoming the first to land near the south pole. Chandrayaan-3's rover "ramped down" from the lander and "India took a walk on the Moon!", the space agency said. The Vikram lander successfully touched down as planned on Wednesday evening. With this, India joins an elite club of countries to achieve a soft landing on the Moon, after the US, the former Soviet Union and China.
